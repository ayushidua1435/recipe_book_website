Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Milk Solids
  • Rose Water
  • Cardmom Powder

Recipe

  1. Boil sugar and water to prepare syrup remove from fire. Add cardamom and keep aside to cool.
  2. In a mixing bowl put the flour, paneer, sooji, Nestlé MILKMAID, baking powder and baking soda. Mix gently to make a soft dough. Do not knead too much.
  3. Divide the mixture into 30-35 portions and gently roll into round gulab jamuns. Fry in the oil at very low flame till golden brown in color.
  4. Keep putting the fried gulab jamuns in the prepared sugar syrup. Once all the gulab jamuns are in the sugar syrup bring it to a boil and remove from flame.

Rasgulla

Ingredients

  • 2 litre milk
  • 1.5 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon rose water
  • 3 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon all purpose flour

Recipe

  1. Heat milk in a pan. Remove from heat once it comes to a boil. Add lemon juice.
  2. Strain the milk in a muslin cloth and collect the cheese. Run the cheese under cold water to remove the sourness from the lemon.
  3. Take out the cheese on the work surface and knead for 4-5 minute till it becomes smooth. Make 10-12 small balls from the cheese.
  4. Heat water, sugar and cardamon in a pan. When the water comes to a boil, add the cheese balls in the water. Cook covered for 40-45 minutes on low heat.
